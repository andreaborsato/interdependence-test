INTERDEPENDENCE @ GARAGE GORIZIA
Design is Happiness

OPENING: Sunday, 14 April 2024, 19.00 (only by invitation)

LOCATION: Garage Gorizia, viale Gorizia 14, Milan

DATES: From 14 to 21 April 2024

"Design is Happiness" showcases the works of students from the Landscape and Interior Spatial Design workshop led by professors Michele De Lucchi, Francesca Balena Arista, Marco De Santi, and Francesco Clerici of the School of Design at Politecnico di Milano. The students have designed and created suspended models and short videos reflecting their thoughts on the theme of Happiness.
